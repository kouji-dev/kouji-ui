A single `provideKjLocale({ locale, direction, calendar })` (or the implicit `Intl.Locale` resolved from the browser) propagates through DI. Every locale-sensitive primitive picks it up — no per-component locale prop drilling. Defaults to `Intl.DateTimeFormat` so anything Angular's `LOCALE_ID` already supports works out of the box.
